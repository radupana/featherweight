- Follow structured training programs from coaches or online sources

**Core Philosophy**: Intelligent automation where it helps, detailed tracking where it matters, and
the flexibility to train however you want.

## How It Works

### Starting a Workout

Open the app and choose your training style:

- **Freestyle**: Add exercises as you go during your workout
- **Program**: Follow the next scheduled workout from your active program
- **Template**: Use a previously saved workout template

### Importing a Program

1. Find any training program online (website, PDF, text document)
2. Copy the program text and paste it into Featherweight
3. The app automatically parses the text and creates structured workouts
4. Map any unrecognized exercise names to the app's database
5. Start following your program immediately

### During Your Workout

- Log each set with weight and reps
- Use the built-in rest timer between sets
- Add personal notes for any exercise
- The app tracks your workout duration automatically

### After Your Workout

- Get notified immediately if you hit any new personal records
- View your workout summary with total volume and duration
- Save the workout as a template for future use
- See your progress updated in real-time charts

### Tracking Your Progress

- **History Screen**: Calendar view of all your completed workouts
- **Insights Screen**: Visual charts of your training patterns and volume
- **Personal Records**: Complete dashboard of all your PRs
- **1RM Tracking**: Monitor your strength progression over time

## What Makes It Different

**The AI Program Parser** is the standout feature - most apps require you to manually build
programs exercise by exercise or use pre-built templates. Featherweight lets you bring _any_
program into the app with minimal effort, whether it's from your coach, a website, or a training
book.

**Automatic PR Detection** creates an engaging experience by celebrating your achievements as they
happen, rather than just storing data.

**Flexible Training Style** supports both structured program followers and freestyle lifters who
prefer to create workouts on the fly.
